Heating with Humidification

An air conditioning system takes in outdoor air at T1°C and %RH1 relative humidity at a steady rate of V1 (m3/time) and conditions to T3°C, %RH3 relative humidity.

The outdoor air is first heated to T2°C in the heating section and then humidified by the injection of hot steam in the humidifying section. Assume the whole process takes place at P(kPa).
