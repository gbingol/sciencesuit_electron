Evaporative Cooling

Air enters an evaporative cooler at 101 (kPa), T1°C and RH1% relative humidity, and it exits at RH2% relative humidity. Determine

  1. The exit temperature of the air,
  2. The lowest temperature to which the air can be cooled by this evaporative cooler.
Hint

Assume that during cooling wet-bulb temperature and enthalpy are constant.

 

Reference: Cengel YA, Boles MA, Kanaoglu M (2019). Thermodynamics: an engineering approach, 9th edition, McGraw-Hill Education.
