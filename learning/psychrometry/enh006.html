HUMIDITY PARAMETERS INVOLVING SATURATION

The following definitions of humidity parameters involve the concept of moist air saturation (1):

Saturation humidity ratio (Ws) :
Humidity ratio of moist air saturated with respect to water (or ice) at the same temperature (T) and pressure (P).

Degree of saturation (m) :
The ratio of the air humidity ratio W to the humidity ratio Ws of saturated air at the same temperature and pressure.
