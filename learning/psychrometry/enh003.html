THERMODYNAMIC PROPERTIES OF WATER AT SATURATION

For the determination of psychrometric properties, knowledge of the water-vapour saturation-pressure is essential. The equations for calculating saturation pressures for temperature ranges -100°C to 200°C are given in Table 2. The solutions for the saturations vapour pressure as a function of temperatures were found with REGAN, using polynomial REGression ANalysis on data obtained from ASHRAE. In some cases however, the saturation pressure was known allowing temperature to be calculated data. In such cases, temperature calculated using saturation pressures where derived from regression analysis are shown in Table 3 (2-4).
